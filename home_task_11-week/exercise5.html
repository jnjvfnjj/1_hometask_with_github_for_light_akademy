CAA Swimming & Diving Weekly Awards

Published on: November 26, 2024

Awards Summary

Delaware's Matvei Namakonov and Angi Ragazzoni received CAA weekly swimming awards, while UNCW's Ethan Badrian and Towson's Shannon Parrau earned CAA weekly diving accolades.

Highlights

Matvei Namakonov (Men’s Swimmer of the Week): The junior from Delaware, originally from Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan, broke three school records at the Bison Invitational, including a 100 breast time of 51.63, ranking 13th nationally.
Angi Ragazzoni (Women’s Swimmer of the Week): Ragazzoni set two program records in the 100 back and 200 back, contributing to five first-place finishes for Delaware.
Ethan Badrian (Men’s Diver of the Week): UNCW’s Badrian swept both diving events at the Patriot Invitational.
Shannon Parrau (Women’s Diver of the Week): Towson’s Parrau excelled on both diving boards, scoring above 280 points in each event.
